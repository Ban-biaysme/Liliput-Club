THE LILIPUT CLUB STORY

Liliput Club was launched in New Zealand in 2009 to meet the gap in the market for quality child care programmes that offer flexible booking arrangements for busy parents. Our mission is to create a home away from home that kids love coming to, and that parents can trust to provide a caring and safe environment.

Our programmes revolve around activities like baking and cooking, sports and games, arts and crafts, science and discovery, along with structured free play and time to relax. We build a strong relationship with our partner schools, who we work closely with to ensure that Liliput Club is a great extension for the school and community. Our vision is that children throughout New Zealand will have the opportunity to experience a Liliput Club programme.
